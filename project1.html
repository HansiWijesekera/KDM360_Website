Truly Sri Lankan



Sri Lankans always had an amazing culture, when it comes to Religion, Family, Health, Administration and Food, however after the invasion of the Portuguese, Dutch and British our citizens have adapted to different lifestyles. It is a very beautiful thing to see and experience. However in the meantime we shouldn't lose our culture, Because that's our identity. As many have chosen to manifest our culture through different platforms/industries, we thought to choose the Food and Beverage industry to manifest our culture, and we are successfully aiming to do it through the 'Truly Sri Lankan' project.
